FAQ

Why can't I come and look at the goods?

Because we are a purely online store and do not have a store building. If you want to pre-inspect the product, we recommend that you look at the reviews on the product pages or in user reviews.

Do you build a computer?

Unfortunately, at the moment we are not assembling the computer, but you can use the Quick Craft service. That will allow you to find out the total price of all components for any filling. And after that you can just use any other build service.

How much does Express delivery cost?

It depends on the delivery address, if the delivery address is not far from the warehouse, then it is possible to make free express delivery. You will learn more about the price when placing an order.

I found damage to the goods after the courier left, where should I contact?

Check the goods upon delivery in the interests of the courier too. Therefore, we ask you to discuss with him all the damage you notice on time. Otherwise, you can contact us directly and we will solve the issue as soon as possible.

Why can't I pick up the goods from the warehouse?

The fact is that the warehouse does not have a place for checking the goods, and the check may cause inconvenience. It will be easier if you check the goods at your comfort at home.

Why do I have to provide email when filling out the support form?

The fact is that we do not use phone numbers to contact you, as this may happen at an inconvenient time for you. And in order to minimize the inconvenience of time during communication, we reply to you on the mail
