About us
PCraft or PC CRaft is a new company selling components and assembling computers. If you are one of the few who want to assemble the PC yourself, and not buy ready-made options, then we will be happy to help you with this. On our site you can either find individual components or quickly assemble a full-fledged PC with any filling of your choice.
Unfortunately, at the moment, services are provided only on the territory of Nur-Sultan, but soon expansion of services is expected throughout Kazakhstan.
