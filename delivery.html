Delivery Information

Door Delivery

Our company provides you with free courier door delivery. Delivery is carried out within two to three work days, in the interval from 17:00 to 19:00.

Express Delivery

We also have a payable express delivery service. Delivery is carried out on the day of the ordering, provided that it is a work day, otherwise delivery will be carried out on the next work day. Express delivery is carried out in the interval from 19:00 to 20:00.
